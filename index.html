Why anaCalypto?

Derived from the Greek word for "discovery," our mission is to uncover the hidden gems of Wilmington—from small food pantries to major cultural events. We believe information is the first step toward community growth.

  • Verified: Every resource is reviewed for accuracy.
  • Inclusive: Focus on services for all residents.
  • Live: Real-time updates on local programs.
